Recreation of the Legend of Zelda's first dungeon with a new shotgun mechanic.

Controls:

Movement: Arrow Keys, WASD

Primary Weapon (Sword): X

Secondary Weapon: Z

Swap Weapons: Space Bar

Toggle Cheats: 1

Switch to Novel Scene: 4

Contributions:

I developed the room transitioning system along with the locked doors system. I also worked on movable blocks and implemented the old man room, bow room, and audio effects. Additionally, implemented the Keese enemy, map collisions, cheats feature, and moving collisions.

Used Unity and C# to develop the game alongside assets provided from course website and from https://www.spriters-resource.com/
